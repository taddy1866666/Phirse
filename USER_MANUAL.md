## SYSTEM SUMMARY

PHIRSE is an Android-based web order management system developed for the Supreme Student Council (SSC) of Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela (PLV). The system allows students to browse and order products from various organizations such as merchandise, t-shirts, and event tickets using an intuitive online platform. It also integrates multiple payment methods including GCash and cash payment options, helping students make convenient purchasing decisions. The system incorporates essential features such as appointment scheduling, order tracking, and notification management into a single unified platform. Administrators can efficiently manage products, orders, students, and organizations through a dedicated admin interface. Organization presidents can manage their product catalogs, track student orders, and monitor sales performance. Overall, PHIRSE enhances student experience while improving operational efficiency and digital workflow within the SSC and student organizations at PLV.

## GETTING STARTED

### STEP 1: ACCESS THE SYSTEM
1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to the PHIRSE URL provided by your administrator
3. The login page will appear

### STEP 2: LOGIN
1. Enter your email address in the "Email" field
2. Enter your password in the "Password" field
3. Click the "Login" button
4. Upon successful login, you will be redirected to your role-specific dashboard

### EXAMPLE LOGIN CREDENTIALS BY USER ROLE:

**ADMIN (Supreme Student Council Staff)**
- Email: admin@plv.edu.ph
- Password: [Provided by SSC administrator]
- Access: Full system access, all management features

**SELLER (Organization President)**
- Email: president@organization.edu.ph
- Password: [Provided by SSC admin during account creation]
- Access: Organization dashboard, product and order management

**STUDENT**
- Email: student@student.edu.ph
- Password: [Provided by student during registration]
- Access: Browse products, place orders, upload payments

*Note: Each user will have their own unique email and password assigned by the system administrator or created during registration.*

### STEP 3: FIRST-TIME USERS
- If you don't have an account, contact your organization administrator
- Ensure your browser allows cookies and JavaScript
- Clear your browser cache if you experience login issues

### STEP 4: PASSWORD RESET
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page (if available)
2. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email
3. Create a new password and log in again

## ACCOUNT AND DATA MANAGEMENT

### ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT - ADMIN (SUPREME STUDENT COUNCIL)

**User Account Management:**

1. **Creating New Accounts**
   - Navigate to Students Management or Organizations Management
   - Click "Add New Account" or "Create Account"
   - Enter email, name, role, and organization (if applicable)
   - System generates temporary password
   - Send credentials to new user
   - User can change password on first login

2. **Editing Account Information**
   - Go to Students/Organizations list
   - Search for user by name or email
   - Click "Edit" to modify details
   - Update information as needed
   - Click "Save Changes"
   - Changes take effect immediately

3. **Deactivating or Deleting Accounts**
   - Select user from list
   - Click "Deactivate Account" or "Delete Account"
   - Confirm action
   - User can no longer access system
   - Data remains in system for records

4. **Resetting User Passwords**
   - Find user in system
   - Click "Reset Password"
   - System sends password reset link to user's email
   - User follows link and creates new password

**Data Viewing and Export:**

1. **View All Student Data**
   - Student names, email addresses, enrollment status
   - Order history and activity
   - Payment verification status
   - Search and filter capabilities

2. **View All Organization Data**
   - Organization names, presidents, contact info

   - Products catalog
   - Student enrollment numbers

3. **Export Data Options**
   - Export to Excel or PDF
   - Select date ranges for reports
   - Choose which columns to include
   - Save for external analysis

**Backup and Data Protection:**
- System automatically backs up data daily
- Access backup settings in System Settings
- Can manually backup important data
- Data encryption for security

1. **Review System Activity Log**

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### COMMON ISSUES AND SOLUTIONS

**ISSUE 1: CANNOT LOGIN**

*Symptoms:* Login fails with error message

*Solutions:*
1. Verify your email address is correct
2. Check your password (case-sensitive)
3. Clear browser cookies and try again
4. Use "Forgot Password" to reset your password
5. Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers)
6. Try a different web browser
7. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in browser settings

*Contact Support If:* Problem persists after trying all steps

---

**ISSUE 2: PAGE LOADING SLOWLY OR NOT LOADING**

*Symptoms:* Blank pages, slow response, timeouts

*Solutions:*
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
3. Clear browser cache:
   - Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+Delete
   - Firefox: Ctrl+Shift+Delete
   - Safari: Command+Option+E
4. Disable browser extensions
5. Try a different browser
6. Wait a few minutes and try again
7. Check if the system is under maintenance

*Contact Support If:* Issue continues after clearing cache

---

**ISSUE 3: CANNOT UPLOAD FILE**

*Symptoms:* File upload fails, payment proof won't upload

*Solutions:*
1. Check file size (usually max 5MB)
2. Verify file format (JPG, PNG, PDF)
3. Use a different image/file
4. Clear browser cookies
5. Try uploading from a different browser
6. Ensure stable internet connection
7. Disable VPN or proxy if using one

*Contact Support If:* Still unable to upload after trying steps

---

**ISSUE 4: PAYMENT NOT VERIFIED**

*Symptoms:* Payment uploaded but status still pending

*Solutions:*
1. Wait 24-48 hours for admin verification
2. Check that receipt shows SSC GCash QR code clearly
3. Verify the amount matches your order total
4. Ensure the receipt photo is clear and legible
5. Check notification tab for admin messages
6. Contact the seller/organization for status

*Contact Support If:* Payment not verified after 48 hours

---

**ISSUE 5: ORDER STATUS NOT UPDATING**

*Symptoms:* Order stuck on same status, no updates

*Solutions:*
1. Refresh the page to reload data
2. Clear browser cache
3. Log out and log back in
4. Check if seller has updated the status
5. Look for notifications about the order
6. Contact the seller for status confirmation
7. Try accessing from a different device

*Contact Support If:* Status doesn't update for extended period

---

**ISSUE 6: NOTIFICATIONS NOT RECEIVED**

*Symptoms:* Missing order updates, payment alerts not showing

*Solutions:*
1. Check notification settings in profile
2. Verify email notifications are enabled
3. Check spam/junk email folder
4. Clear browser cache and refresh
5. Log out and log back in
6. Check browser notification permissions
7. Verify email address is correct in profile

*Contact Support If:* Still not receiving notifications

---

**ISSUE 7: CANNOT FIND PRODUCT**

*Symptoms:* Cannot locate product in catalog, search not working

*Solutions:*
1. Try different search keywords
2. Check if product is in stock
3. Filter by category and organization
4. Clear search filters and try again
5. Refresh the products page
6. Check if product might be archived
7. Contact the organization/seller

*Contact Support If:* Product should exist but cannot find it

---

**ISSUE 8: SLOW PERFORMANCE ON MOBILE**

*Symptoms:* App slow on phone, buttons unresponsive

*Solutions:*
1. Close other apps and browser tabs
2. Check mobile internet connection (use WiFi if possible)
3. Update your browser to latest version
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Reduce image quality in browser settings
6. Try landscape orientation for better view
7. Restart your mobile device

*Contact Support If:* Performance issues persist

---

### GENERAL TROUBLESHOOTING TIPS
- **Clear Browser Cache:** Often resolves display and loading issues
- **Update Your Browser:** Keep to latest version for security and compatibility
- **Disable Extensions:** Some extensions can interfere with the system
- **Use Supported Browsers:** Stick to Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
- **Check Internet Connection:** Ensure stable connection
- **Check System Status:** Visit the system status page for maintenance notices
- **Restart Your Device:** Simple but often effective
- **Use Private/Incognito Mode:** Tests if extensions are causing issues

### FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

**Q: How long does order processing take?**
A: Orders are typically processed within 24-48 hours after payment verification. Sellers will update the status accordingly.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: You can cancel orders that are still in "Pending" status. Contact the seller for further assistance with other statuses.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, all payments are processed securely. We use encrypted connections and do not store credit card information.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions sent to your registered email address.

**Q: What payment methods are available?**
A: Currently, we support cash payment (in-person) and GCash digital payment.

**Q: How do I report a problem with my order?**
A: Contact the seller/organization directly through the system or reach out to SSC support with your order number.
