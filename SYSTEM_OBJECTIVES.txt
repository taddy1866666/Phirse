PHIRSE - SSC ORDER MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
SYSTEM OBJECTIVES & FEATURE IMPLEMENTATION REPORT

================================================================================
1. OBJECTIVE OF THE STUDY
================================================================================

1.1 GENERAL OBJECTIVES

This study aims to develop a website for the Supreme Student Council (SSC) of 
Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela (PLV) to enhance the reservation and order 
process for various items and improve efficiency in managing transactions, as 
well as to integrate an online payment method which the school is currently lacking.

1.2 SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES

To design and develop a reservation and order management platform for the Supreme 
Student Council (SSC) of PLV that will automate order reservations, stock tracking, 
and transaction management while providing real-time monitoring and comprehensive 
reporting capabilities.

================================================================================
7. KEY WORKFLOWS
================================================================================

7.1 ORDER PLACEMENT WORKFLOW
1. Student browses products
2. Student selects product and places order
3. System creates order record
4. Student uploads payment receipt (GCash)
5. Admin verifies payment
6. Seller receives notification and processes order
7. Order status updates automatically
8. Student receives notification

7.2 PRODUCT MANAGEMENT WORKFLOW
1. Seller adds new product with images and details
2. Product submitted for admin approval
3. Admin reviews and approves/rejects
4. Seller receives notification
5. Approved products appear in student portal
6. Seller manages inventory in real-time

7.3 PAYMENT VERIFICATION WORKFLOW
1. Student uploads payment receipt with GCash QR
2. Admin receives notification
3. Admin verifies payment authenticity
4. System updates payment status
5. Seller & Student receive notifications
6. Order processing continues
